⚠️ Why phone photos alone aren't enough
A photo on your phone has no independent verification. The timestamp can be altered, metadata edited, and the file modified without any trace. Rental companies already know this — their own systems use cryptographic hashing and server-side timestamps. Pruff gives every consumer the exact same standard: every photo is hashed, server-timestamped, GPS-tagged, and locked the moment it is taken. It cannot be altered by anyone. That is the difference between a photo and proof.
